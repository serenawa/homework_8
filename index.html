a little about me...

In my free time I love baking because the process is soothing and it’s so rewarding to see a beautiful and delicious finished product. It makes me so happy to see people enjoy the desserts I’ve spent time and care into making. Whenever I start a new bake, I always read a few recipes before synthesizing the best ones to achieve the desired outcome.

In this mini-site, you can find my favorite treats and interactive recipes to learn how to make them from scratch. Many recipe sites are hard to follow along because the information is presented in a linear manner, forcing you to constantly scroll to remember ingredient proportions, demonstration images, and tips. This site improves information visualization for recipes so it’s easy for you to follow along step-by-step. Bon appetit!
